This is your Notagochi!

(and it loves you...)



Welcome to the land of Notagotchi. They are lovable creatures that need food, sleep, and fun to grow and live long, enjoyable lives.

As your pet, your Notagotchi needs each of its three basic needs; food, sleep, and play, every five minutes. Thankfully it takes no time at all to play, feed, or put your Nota-gotchi to bed.

Your Notagotchi will grow an age every seven minutes. See it transform and grow over time! Your Notagotchi will also alert you when it needs something. Eventually, your Notagotchi will age and pass, but a new egg will be waiting for you!
