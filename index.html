This is your Nota-gochi!

(and it loves you...)



Welcome to the land of Nota-gotchi. They are lovable creatures that need food, sleep, and fun to grow and live long, enjoyable lives.

As your pet, your Nota-gotchi needs each of its three basic needs; food, sleep, and play, every five minutes. Thankfully it takes no time at all to play, feed, or put your Nota-gotchi to bed.

Your Nota-gotchi will grow an age every seven minutes. See it transform and grow over time! Your Nota-gotchi will also alert you when it needs something. Eventually, your Nota-gotchi will age and pass, but a new egg will be waiting for you!
